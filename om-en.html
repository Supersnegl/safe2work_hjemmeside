About us

Safe 2 Work ApS was founded in 2019 by Peter Misander. Since the beginning, the company has worked to integrate safety as a natural part of the design and development process, as well as making risk assessments easier to understand and more accessible.

Safe 2 Work today consists of three safety engineers with competencies in chemistry, construction/fire safety and mechanical engineering, as well as a student assistant. We have offices at DTU Science Park and are closely connected to the DTU environment, enabling us to continuously stay updated on the latest knowledge within safety. We strive to solve tasks efficiently and work continuously to develop and improve our digital tools, so we can deliver the best possible service to our customers.

Safe 2 Work works with:

  • Helping customers reduce accident risks to an acceptable level for employees, visitors and neighbors of a facility.
  • Helping customers achieve compliance with regulations and requirements within safety, working environment, fire protection, emergency preparedness, etc.
  • Helping customers gain an overview of – and develop strategies for – safety issues, preferably as early in the project as possible.
