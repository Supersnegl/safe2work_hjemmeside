Safe 2 Work ApS is a consulting engineering company

Our core competences is risk, safety and working environment in industry. We help companies make plants, machinery and work processes safe, well thought-out and robust in practice – preventing incidents and workplace accidents.

Our motto is: “Where ever you work, we make it safe 2 work”. We thrive when solving complex safety challenges and translating requirements and analyses into concrete solutions that work in everyday operations.

Contact us More about us

At Safe 2 Work, we work broadly with industrial safety – from strategy and management systems to concrete analyses, design and operations. Our services include, among others:

  • Technical safety in design and operation – safety by design, barriers/safety functions, requirements specifications and verification.
  • Process Safety & risk analyses – HAZID/HAZOP, consequence modelling and QRA, as well as risk documentation for high-risk companies.
  • Functional Safety (SIS/IEC 61511) – LOPA, SRS, SIL verification, testing and commissioning of safety instrumented functions.
  • ATEX, fire and explosion safety – zone classification, ATEX risk assessments/inspections, fire reviews and explosion calculations/modelling.
  • CE marking & technical compliance – Machinery Directive/Regulation, ATEX and PED (strategy, technical file and conformity).
  • Practical implementation – procedures and emergency preparedness, work permits/LOTO, PSS and inspections, as well as chemical safety (CLP), risk assessments and working environment initiatives.
